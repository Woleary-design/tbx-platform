# TBX Hero Direction

## Direction

The current hero direction should stay bright, premium and playful. It should not become dark, serious, or corporate.

## What works

- Warm museum-white background
- Large, confident headline
- TBX yellow as the emotional colour
- The cabinet as the signature visual idea
- Playful floating brick moments
- Trust-first positioning

## What needs improvement

The current coded cabinet is too flat and abstract. It should not use simple geometric placeholder shapes.

The cabinet should feel like a bright collector display case with original brick-built models, warm lighting, glass, soft shadows and depth.

## Cabinet requirements

- Bright cream / white cabinet, not dark brown
- Warm spotlights in each compartment
- Rounded premium furniture details
- Six compartments
- One yellow-highlighted compartment
- Original brick-built inspired models only
- No copyrighted set recreations
- No LEGO minifigures
- No simple triangles or clip-art shapes

## Hero mood

80% premium, 20% wonder.

Think Apple × collector museum × joyful building blocks.

## Copy

Primary headline:

Own with confidence.
Trade with trust.

Supporting copy:

Rare sets, sealed boxes and collector pieces traded with verification, reputation and TBX Secure protection.

## Primary CTAs

- Browse Marketplace
- View Vault

## Visual standard

The homepage should feel like a bright collector museum, not an admin dashboard and not a toy-shop website.
